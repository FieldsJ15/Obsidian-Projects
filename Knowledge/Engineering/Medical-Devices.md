# Medical Devices

## Categories
- **Diagnostic** — [[Medical-Imaging]] devices, [[EEG]] machines, ECG
- **Therapeutic** — [[TMS]], [[tDCS]], linac (radiotherapy), HIFU
- **Implantable** — pacemakers, DBS, cochlear implants
- **Wearables** — continuous glucose monitors, smartwatch ECG
- **Surgical** — robotic surgery (Da Vinci), navigation systems

## Regulation
- FDA (USA): 510(k), PMA
- CE Mark (Europe): MDR 2017/745
- ISO 13485 — quality management for medical devices
- IEC 60601 — electrical safety

## Design Considerations
- Biocompatibility (ISO 10993)
- Sterilization
- EMC / interference
- Usability / human factors
