# Medical Imaging

> Acquiring visual representations of the body for clinical or research purposes.

## Modalities
| Modality | What it measures | Resolution | Radiation |
|----------|-----------------|------------|-----------|
| [[MRI]] | Tissue water/fat, function | High | No |
| [[CT]] | X-ray attenuation (density) | High | Yes |
| [[PET]] | Metabolic activity | Low-Med | Yes |
| [[SPECT]] | Blood flow / receptor binding | Low | Yes |
| [[Ultrasound]] | Sound reflection | Med | No |

## Key Concepts
- **DICOM** → [[DICOM]] — universal format for all modalities
- **Registration** — aligning images from different modalities
- **Segmentation** — delineating structures (tumors, organs)
- **Reconstruction** — from raw signal to image
