# SPECT — Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography

## Physics
- Radiotracer emits single gamma ray
- Rotating gamma camera detects photons
- Lower resolution than [[PET]], cheaper, more accessible

## vs PET
| | SPECT | PET |
|---|---|---|
| Photons | 1 | 2 (coincidence) |
| Resolution | Lower | Higher |
| Cost | Lower | Higher |
| Availability | Wide | Less common |
| Tracers | Tc-99m, I-123 | F-18, C-11 |

## Clinical Uses
- **Brain perfusion** — stroke, dementia
- **Cardiac** — myocardial perfusion (most common SPECT scan)
- **Bone scan** — metastases, fractures
- **Thyroid** — function, cancer
