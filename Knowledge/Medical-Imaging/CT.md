# CT — Computed Tomography

## Physics
- X-ray beam rotates around patient
- Attenuation measured → back-projection reconstruction
- Output: Hounsfield Units (HU) — tissue density scale
  - Air: -1000 HU | Water: 0 HU | Bone: +400 to +1000 HU

## Clinical Uses
- Trauma, emergency (fast)
- Chest: lung nodules, PE, aorta
- Abdomen: liver, kidneys, bowel
- **Radiotherapy planning** — primary modality → [[MLC-Sim]]
- Head: hemorrhage, fractures

## Key Concepts
- **CBCT** (Cone Beam CT) — used in radiotherapy for daily positioning
- **Low-dose CT** — lung cancer screening
- **Contrast CT** — iodine contrast for vessels/organs
