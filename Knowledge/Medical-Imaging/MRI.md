# MRI — Magnetic Resonance Imaging

## Physics
- Nuclear magnetic resonance of hydrogen atoms
- T1, T2, T2* relaxation times
- k-space acquisition → Fourier transform → image
- Field strengths: 1.5T, 3T, 7T (research)

## Sequences
- **T1** — anatomy, fat bright
- **T2** — pathology, fluid bright
- **FLAIR** — suppress CSF, show lesions
- **DWI** — diffusion, stroke detection
- **fMRI** — blood oxygenation (BOLD signal) → brain function
- **MRS** — spectroscopy, metabolite concentrations

## Clinical Uses
- Brain: tumors, MS, stroke → [[Neuromodulation]], [[TMS]]
- Spine, joints, soft tissue
- Cardiac MRI
- Prostate, liver
