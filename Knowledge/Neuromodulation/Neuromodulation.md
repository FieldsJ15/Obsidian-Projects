# Neuromodulation

> Altering neural activity through targeted stimulation — electrical, magnetic, or chemical.

## Non-Invasive
- [[TMS]] — Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation
- [[tDCS]] — Transcranial Direct Current Stimulation
- tACS — Transcranial Alternating Current Stimulation
- Ultrasound neuromodulation (FUS)

## Invasive
- DBS — Deep Brain Stimulation (Parkinson's, OCD)
- Spinal cord stimulation
- Vagus nerve stimulation
- Intracranial EEG

## Monitoring
- [[EEG]] — primary readout of neuromodulation effects
- [[MRI]] (fMRI) — spatial mapping of effects
- [[SPECT]], [[PET]] — neurotransmitter changes

## Clinical Applications
- Depression (TMS — FDA approved)
- OCD, PTSD
- Parkinson's, essential tremor (DBS)
- Chronic pain
- Stroke rehabilitation
- Cognitive enhancement (research)
